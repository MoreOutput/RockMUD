Player Classes

Classes define the main attribute and base skills for your character. Skills can also be obtained from in game items, trainers and quests. A classes main attribute has a reduced training cost and factors into various game checks.

Name Main Stat About
Fighter Strength Focuses on melee combat skills and weapon knowledge.
Mage Intelligence Uses Mana to cast powerful arcane spells.
Thief Dexterity Uses movement and dexterity to master attacking from the shadows.
Cleric Wisdom Excels at using spells and rituals requring life and mana to heal others and deal damage to the undead.
Ranger Constitution Expert hunters, survivalists, and trappers.

Skills are also obtained through in game quests and items. Generally intelligence based classes will never be as good at a physical skill as a melee oriented class and vice versa.
