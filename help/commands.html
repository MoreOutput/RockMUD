Command Help

Overview of RockMUD current commands. No command is case sensitive.

North, East, South, West, Up, Down
General movement directions. All possible directions map to the move command.
Aliases: n, r, s, w, u, d respectively.
Look [target]
Look closely at something. If you do not give a target the current room is selected.
Ex: look
Score
Your character stats and affects.
Inventory
Held items.
Aliases: i
Equipment
View worn equipment and open slots.
Aliases: eq
Stand
Stop sleeping or resting and return to the 'default' position.
Rest
Take a resting position for increased regeneration. Cannot perform some actions.
Sleep
Begin to sleep to greatly increase regeneration.
Open [direction]
Open a closed door/pathway in a given direction. Giving the command a target will also work.
Ex: open down OR open gate
Close [direction]
Close an opened door/pathway in a given direction. Giving the command a target will also work.
Ex: close down OR close gate
Unlock [direction]
Unlock a pathway once you have the key in your inventory.
Ex: unlock gate
Lock [direction]
Lock a pathway once you have the key in your inventory.
Ex: lock gate
Time
Current mud time and status.
Who
All visible players.
Title [new title]
The words that appear after your characters name on the score card and who window. Defaults to race and class.
Get [item]
Pick up a target item in the current room or target container. Use get all to retrieve all of the items on the floor.
Ex: get bread OR get key bag
Drop [item]
Drop a target item.
Ex: drop bread OR drop all
Wear [item]
Wear a target item. It must be in your inventory
Ex: wear sword
Remove [item]
Remove a target item. It must be in your inventory.
Ex: remove sword
Move [direction]
General movement command. The other movement commands are aliases of move.
Ex: move north
Say
Send a message to everyone in the current room.
Ex: say hello
Yell
Send a message to everyone in your current area.
Ex: yell hello
Chat
General mud-wide communication channel.
Ex: chat hello
Kill [target]
Begin combat with a target.
Alias: k boar
Flee [direction]
Try to flee from battle. Name a direction or it will be randomized.
Ex: flee
Cast [spell name] [target]
Cast a spell. If you're already fighting you do not need to name the target.
Ex: cast spark boar
Emote [message to room]
Emote an action to the room.
Ex: emote nods
Eat [food item in inventory]
Ex: eat pie
Drink [liquid in inventory or room]
Ex: drink waterskin
Scan
Glance at adjacent rooms.
